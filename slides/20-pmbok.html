Connection to PMBOK Guide

Uncertainty Performance Domain
  • System Errors: Inherent sources of uncertainty—you cannot foresee all bugs.
  • FMEA: Proactive risk identification before problems happen.
  • RCA: Learning from past failures to reduce future uncertainties.
  • Risk Matrix & Register: Quantifying vague "risk feelings" into comparable ratings and managing them systematically.
Applying these tools embodies the PMBOK principles: Navigate Complexity and Optimize Risk Responses.
