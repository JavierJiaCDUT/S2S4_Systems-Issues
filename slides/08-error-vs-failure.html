Error vs Failure

Attribute Error Failure
Definition Flaws in code, design, or operation System fails to provide the required service
Nature Root Cause Manifestation (Symptom)
Visibility May lie dormant in code, un-triggered Directly perceived by the user
Relationship Error is the CAUSE Failure is the CONSEQUENCE
Key Insight: Not all Errors lead to Failures. Some may never be triggered. But behind every Failure, there is always at least one Error.
