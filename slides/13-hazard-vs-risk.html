Hazard vs Risk

Attribute Hazard Risk
Definition A condition or state that can cause harm. The combination of Likelihood and Impact.
Nature Static — it "exists" there. Dynamic — considers "probability".
Example Self-driving system fails to detect pedestrians in rain. Deploying this system in a rainy city (Probability × Fatalities).
Analogy A cliff with no guardrails. 1000 people walking past that cliff every day.
