Risk Response Strategies

Strategy Description Example
Avoid Change plan entirely to eliminate risk. Drop autonomous driving; switch to driver assist.
Mitigate Reduce the likelihood or impact of the risk. Add 2-factor authentication to payment system.
Transfer Shift risk to a third party. Buy cybersecurity insurance or use external API.
Accept Acknowledge risk exists, prepare contingencies. Low-impact edge cases: record and monitor.
