Methodology

FMEA (Failure Mode and Effects Analysis)

Core idea: Before problems happen, anticipate "What could go wrong? What are the consequences? How do we prevent it?"

S
Severity (1-10)
How bad are the consequences?
O
Occurrence (1-10)
How likely is it to happen?
D
Detection (1-10)
How likely are we to detect it?
RPN = Severity × Occurrence × Detection

(Risk Priority Number) - The higher the RPN, the higher the priority to fix.
