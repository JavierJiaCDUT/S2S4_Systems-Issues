Evolution

The Complete Chain

A system problem becomes more serious as it moves from a hidden defect, to a visible service problem, to a dangerous condition, and finally to a real-world risk.

Start

Error

Code, design, or process flaw exists inside the system.

Example: Old trading logic was not removed during deployment.

Triggered

Failure

The system stops behaving as intended and produces the wrong service outcome.

Example: The old module starts sending unintended trades.

Dangerous State

Hazard

A harmful condition now exists, even if harm has not happened yet.

Example: Large trades can execute automatically without effective control.

Exposure

Risk

The hazard now has a probability and impact in the real world.

Example: In a live market, financial loss becomes highly likely and severe.
